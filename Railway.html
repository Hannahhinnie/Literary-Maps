You find yourself in Haworth Yorkshire far away from the familiarity of London. The Railway Children moved to a placce like this and lived in a place by the railway station called 'The Three Chimneys'.

2

Next, walk to Oakworth station. The coming and going of trains with the crossing gates here parallels that which Mr Perks controls in the book.

3

Now walk by Mr Perk's cottage.

4

Continue along the footpath and you will find the tunnel where Jim gets his leg trapped. On the side of the embankment to your left is where the Railway Children watch the trains go past.
